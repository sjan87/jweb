Hanoi, Vietnam

You can guess it from this rooftop view. Hanoi is a rather chaotic place, but definitely worth a visit.

Shirakawa-go, Japan

Visited this picturesque village during a tour of Japan. Shirakawa-go was declared an UNESCO World Heritage Site.

Broken Bay, Nusa Penida, Indonesia

The Broken Bay on Nusa Penida from a bird's eye view. Besides Kelingking beach, this is a must-see on this island close to Bali.

Tanah Lot, Bali, Indonesia

No filter needed! Stunningly beautiful and surrounded by water during high tide. The temple of Tanah Lot in Bali.

Römer, Frankfurt, Germany

Der Römer is Frankfurt's town hall and, of course, one of its main attractions.

Dallas, USA

Travel tip for Dallas: Enjoy the great view of the skyline from Reunion Tower.

Matterhorn, Zermatt, Switzerland

It took me 30 years before I finally got a view of this iconic mountain. This picture was taken on a hike from Zermatt to Gornergrat.

Bangkok, Thailand

Bangkok by night. Nuff said.

Arc de Triomphe de l’Étoile, Paris, France

Paris' famous triumphal arch even has an observation deck. Unfortunately, I didn't realise that at the time of my visit.

Church of the Resurrection of Christ, St. Petersburg, Russia

A Russian Orthodox church with its characteristic onion domes. Welcome to St. Petersburg!

Chain Bridge, Budapest, Hungary

The Chain Bridge is connecting the two parts of the city, Buda and Pest, making it clear where this photo was taken.

Helsinki, Finland

What a fantastic design. A block of flats in the downtown area of Helsinki.

Golden Gate Bridge, San Francisco, USA

The renowned Golden Gate Bridge is still among the world's longest suspension bridges – being the second longest in the US.

Yosemite Valley, California, USA

Yosemite National Park in California. Not only familiar to Apple users.

Cloud Gate, Chicago, USA

This sight can be found in the Millennium Park in Chicago: The Cloud Gate aka «The Bean».

The Great Sphinx of Giza, Cairo, Egypt

The Great Sphinx of Giza and the pyramid of Chephren in its background.
